Project Description

Endure and Survive is a 2D educational survival game designed to teach players essential wilderness skills through interactive gameplay. The game emphasizes exploration, resource management, and problem-solving in a dynamic environment. Players must gather materials, craft survival tools, and complete challenges across three distinct levels—each focused on a real-world survival skill.

  • Platform: Godot
  • Audience: Middle school students and beginners interested in outdoor survival
  • Development Tools: C# and custom-built physics and game logic

Background & Motivation

The concept of Endure and Survive draws inspiration from the importance of teaching practical survival skills in an engaging and interactive way. Traditional lessons on wilderness safety can often feel abstract or disconnected. This project turns those concepts into hands-on learning experiences by gamifying them—allowing players to learn by doing.

Inspired by outdoor adventures and family traditions of camping and hiking, the team designed the game to capture the thrill and challenge of surviving in nature. The project also encourages teamwork and adaptability, key skills both in outdoor activities and in software development itself.
